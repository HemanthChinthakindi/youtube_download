How to use the tool

  1. Launch the application on your chosen platform.
  2. Provide/Paste your desired YouTube URL into the input field.
  3. Click on Fetch Info to load video data.
  4. Once the info appears, select your preferred resolution for MP4 to download video, or select MP3 if you wish to download only the audio.
